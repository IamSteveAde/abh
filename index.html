Solutions for Strategic Bitcoin Adoption.

Innovative, institution-grade services designed to place African organizations at the center of the global Bitcoin economy, with risk-aware treasury, scalable mining, and resilient infrastructure.

Treasury Management

We provide institution-grade custody and strategic reserve planning for corporations, sovereign entities, and financial institutions. Our offerings include secure multi-sig custody architecture, independent attestations, and layered governance designed to maintain operational continuity under stress scenarios.

Structured accumulation programs (SAP) enable disciplined, periodic acquisition to reduce timing risk and improve long-term reserve quality. We advise on balance-sheet treatment, compliance-ready reporting, and bespoke treasury policies tailored to regulatory and fiduciary constraints.

Bitcoin Mining & Accumulation

We develop and operate large-scale mining facilities powered by low-cost renewable and stranded energy ‚Äî hydro, solar, and gas-to-power projects across the continent. Our engineering and supply-chain teams optimize rack density, thermal design, and energy procurement to deliver world-class hash-per-dollar.

Mining is treated as a long-term accumulation engine: mined Bitcoin is primarily retained on balance sheet to enhance corporate reserves and strategic positioning. We pair operational excellence with transparent reporting and Bitcoin custody solutions so stakeholders can audit performance and reserves with confidence.

Strategic Infrastructure

We build the technical and governance backbone that enables scale: AI-driven treasury dashboards, resilient cold-storage networks, and pooled participation vehicles such as the African Bitcoin Accumulation Fund (ABAF). These products provide visibility, operational controls, and scalable access to Bitcoin exposure.

Beyond tech, we engage policymakers and stakeholders to design pragmatic frameworks that balance innovation with financial stability. Our research and education programs equip executives and regulators with the facts and models to make durable, forward-looking decisions.
